The Project

Authentic Shakespearean Style

Bardify uses a custom-trained GPT-Neo model to generate sonnets that are faithful to the language, structure, and themes of Shakespearean poetry. The model has been fine-tuned on a large corpus of Shakespearean sonnets and plays to ensure that the output is of high quality and consistent with the Bard's style.

Customizable Prompts

Users can input their own prompts or themes for the sonnets, allowing for a wide range of creative possibilities. The website will generate a unique sonnet based on the user's input, providing a personalized and engaging experience.

User-Friendly Interface

Bardify features a sleek and intuitive design, with clear instructions and prompts for generating sonnets. The output is displayed in an attractive and readable format.

Potential Future Extensions

Some Advanced AI features such as sentiment analysis, rhyme detection, and meter detection can be included, to ensure that the generated sonnets are of the highest quality. These features can be toggled on or off depending on the user's preferences, allowing for a customizable and sophisticated experience.
